# Embroidery Designer Panel - Complete Documentation

Welcome to the Embroidery Designer Panel. This guide explains how to use the various features of the platform to manage your designs, track your sales, and handle your earnings.

---

## 1. Authentication & Security
The platform ensures your account is secure with multiple login options.
- **Email Login**: Standard login using your registered email and password.
- **Google Login**: Quick access using your Google account.
- **OTP Verification**: Enhanced security for sensitive actions or new registrations.
- **Forgot Password**: Easily reset your password via email if you lose access.

---

## 2. Dashboard
The **Dashboard** is your home screen. It provides a quick overview of your performance:
- **Total Designs**: How many designs you have uploaded.
- **Pending Approvals**: Designs waiting for admin review.
- **Total Sales**: Your overall sales count.
- **Earnings Summary**: A snapshot of your current balance.

---

## 3. Design Management (Upload & Edit)
This is the core feature of the panel. You can upload various types of designs, each with specific technical details.

### How to Upload a Design:
1. Go to **Upload Design** in the sidebar.
2. Select the **Design Type** first (this is important as it changes the required fields).
3. Fill in the **Basic Information** (Name, Price, Category).
4. Provide **Technical Details** based on the design type (see below).
5. Upload a **Preview Image** and the **Source Files**.
6. Click **Submit for Review**.

### Supported Design Types:
- **Embroidery Design**: Requires Stitching Count, Needle Count, Hoop Size, and files in .DST, .PES, or .EMB formats.
- **Beads Embroidery**: Similar to standard embroidery but includes Bead Size, Bead Type, and Bead Color Count.
- **Sketch Design**: For digital or manual sketches. Includes Sketch Type, Paper Size, and a "Digital" toggle.
- **Jewelry Design**: Specify Jewelry Type (Ring, Necklace, etc.), Metal Type, and Stone details.
- **Hot Fix Design**: For rhinestone patterns. Requires Stone Size (SS), Stone Type, and Stone Count.
- **Textile Design (TDS)**: Focuses on Repeat Type and Fabric Type.
- **Texture Design**: For 3D or material textures. Includes resolution and Map Types (Normal, Diffuse, etc.).
- **Digital Print**: Standard print designs requiring Print Size, Color Mode (CMYK/RGB), and DPI.
- **Print Planning**: For layout and material planning.

## 4. Analytics
Track your success in detail:
- **Sales Trends**: Charts showing your sales over time.
- **Top Performing Designs**: See which designs are earning the most.
- **Category Popularity**: Understand what customers are looking for.

---

## 5. Wallet & Withdrawals
Manage your earnings efficiently:
- **Current Balance**: Your total available earnings.
- **Transaction History**: A detailed log of all sales and withdrawals.
- **Withdrawal Requests**: Request your earnings to be transferred to your account once you reach the minimum threshold.
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor the status of your withdrawal requests (Pending, Approved, Success).

---

## 6. Profile & Settings
Customize your presence on the platform:
- **Profile Page**: Update your bio, shop name, and contact details to build trust with buyers.
- **Account Settings**: Change your password, update notification preferences, and manage security settings.

---

## 7. Best Practices for Designers
- **High-Quality Previews**: Always upload clear, well-lit preview images to attract more buyers.
- **Detailed Descriptions**: Use the description field to explain the inspiration or usage tips for your design.
- **Accurate Metadata**: Ensure technical details like stitch counts and stone sizes are 100% accurate to avoid disputes.
